    Tilty Bird

    Tilty Bird is a new take on the flappy craze that is sweeping the world! Instead of tapping the screen to dodge obstacles, Tilty Bird takes advantage of the device's accelerometer. You must tilt the device to move the bird from side to side.

    Objective

    Help the bird dodge as many obstacles as possible!
    Beat you High Score!

    How to Play

    Tilt device to move bird.
    Try not to hit the pipes that are on the left and right of the screen.
    Have Fun and Don't forget to Rate!
